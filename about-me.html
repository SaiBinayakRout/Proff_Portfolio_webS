About Me

I’m a B.Tech graduate in Electrical & Electronics Engineering who discovered a passion for IT and data during my academic journey. The transition from circuits to code, from logic gates to unlocking the secrets hidden in data, was more than just a career shift—it was a personal evolution.
What excites me is the ability to transform raw, complex datasets into actionable insights that drive better decision-making. Whether analyzing business trends or developing dynamic dashboards, I’ve learned that data is more than numbers—it tells a story, and I’m passionate about deciphering it.
In addition to my academic background, I’ve completed a Master’s course in Data Science & Business Analytics from Simplilearn, where I gained hands-on experience in Excel, MySQL, Data Visualization with Python, Tableau, and completed industry-relevant projects that allowed me to apply these skills in real-world scenarios.
My professional experience includes internships as a Frontend Development Intern at Techstain Technologies Pvt. Ltd. and a Frontend Development Trainee at Smart5Solution. These roles provided me with valuable experience in building user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. More importantly, they ignited my interest in data analysis by revealing the power of the data that drives user experiences.
With technical expertise in Python, MySQL, and Tableau, I approach every project with curiosity and a drive for continuous learning.
Outside of work, I enjoy learning about AI, machine learning, and animation. I'm also passionate about automotive innovation and have recently developed a strong interest in business analysis and development, always seeking ways to combine these fields with my expertise in data.
